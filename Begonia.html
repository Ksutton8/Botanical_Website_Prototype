Wax Begonia

(Begonia cucullata)

Begonia cucullata, commonly known as the wax begonia or angel wing begonia,
is a charming flowering plant appreciated for its waxy, heart-shaped
leaves and pendulous clusters of delicate flowers. With a compact and bushy
growth habit, it thrives in shaded garden areas, providing vibrant color and texture.

Growth Requirements

  • Light: Partial to full shade
  • Soil Type: Loam
  • Soil pH: Slightly acidic (5.5-6.5)
  • Soil Drainage: Good drainage
  • Water: Average

Medicinal Properties

  • Mucilaginous properties that are beneficial for
    soothing skin condition
  • Anti-inflammatory, diuretic, and wound-healing properties
