Ornamental Onion

(Allium hollandicum)

The ornamental onion (Allium) is a visually striking
flowering plant with globe-shaped clusters of tiny, star-shaped
florets in vibrant hues like purple, pink, white, and blue.
Known for its resilience and adaptability, it adds a burst
of color to gardens, attracts pollinators, and stands out as
an elegant, low-maintenance ornamental plant.

Growth Requirements

  • Light: Full sun
  • Soil Type: Clay, loam, sand
  • Soil pH: Alkaline (>8.0),
    Neutral (6.0-8.0)
  • Soil Drainage: Good drainage, moist
  • Water: Average

Medicinal Properties

  • Antibacterial properties that help combat bacterial infections
  • Antioxidant effects that help reduce stress
  • Immune system support that helps the body defend against infections
