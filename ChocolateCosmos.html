Chocolate Cosmos

(Cosmos atrosanguineus)

Cosmos atrosanguineus, often called the chocolate cosmos,
is a captivating flowering plant known for its rich, chocolate-like
fragrance and deep burgundy to almost black-hued blooms. With
velvety petals and a compact growth habit, it adds a delightful
sensory experience to gardens and attracts pollinators. This perennial
beauty thrives in well-drained soil and sunny conditions.

Growth Requirements

  • Light: Direct sun
  • Soil Type: Loam, sand
  • Soil pH: Slightly acidic (5.5-6.0)
  • Soil Drainage: Good drainage
  • Water: Average

Medicinal Properties

The strong chocolate scent of the Chocolate Cosmos flowers
is appreciated for its aromatic qualities contributing
to its use in aromatherapy.
