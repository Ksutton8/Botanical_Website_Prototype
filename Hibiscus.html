Hibiscus

(Hibiscus rosa-sinensis)

Hibiscus rosa-sinensis is a vibrant and iconic flowering plant.
Renowned for its large, showy blossoms in hues of red, pink, orange, yellow, and white,
it adds a tropical flair to gardens and landscapes. This sun-loving shrub is a
favorite for warm climates.

Growth Requirements

  • Light: Direct sun
  • Soil Type: Loam
  • Soil pH: Acidic (6.5-6.8)
  • Soil Drainage: Good drainage
  • Water: Average

Medicinal Properties

  • Rich in antioxidants which help combat stress
  • Helps lower blood pressure and reduce cholestral levels
  • Anti-inflammatory effects
  • Immune boosting properties
