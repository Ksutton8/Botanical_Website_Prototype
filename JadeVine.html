Jade Vine

(Strongylodon macrobotrys)

Strongylodon macrobotrys, commonly known as the jade vine,
is a rare and enchanting tropical plant. Its cascading clusters of turquoise
to teal, claw-shaped flowers make it a botanical treasure. Native
to the Philippines, this vine adds a touch of extraordinary beauty to
gardens and greenhouses, requiring a warm and humid environment to thrive.

Growth Requirements

  • Light: Direct sun
  • Soil Type: Sand
  • Soil pH: Slightly acidic to neutral (6.0-7.0)
  • Soil Drainage: Good drainage
  • Water: Below average

Medicinal Properties

The jade vine is not known for having medicinal propertiesand is
primarily cultivated for its aesthetic appeal. Its unique flowers and lush foliage make it
a sought-after plant for gardens and conservatories.
